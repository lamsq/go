  <h1>About Go</h1>
  <p>
    Go is an ancient board game that traces its origins back thousands of years, predominantly in China, and is especially popular in East Asia. It's played by two players who alternately place black and white stones on the intersections of a grid. Unlike many other board games, Go focuses on surrounding territory rather than capturing pieces outright.
  </p>
  <p>
    Despite its simple rules, Go is incredibly deep and offers vast strategic possibilities. Even small boards present a challenge for beginners and experienced players alike. The game fosters creativity, balance, and intuition, making Go a timeless classic enjoyed worldwide by millions of enthusiasts.
  </p>
